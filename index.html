Worship, Sound doctrine & Financial empowerment.

real Worship is giving from the heart without taking credit

Worship-Mentortainment is a non-profit organization aimed at raising champions in this generation and the generations to come through different forums i.e. worship experiences, business conferences, youth seminars, talent shows, weekend challenges, children’s home visits etc. Through these forums, leaders are developed, destinies are realized, young people are empowered and many lives are transformed through teachings of the balanced doctrine of the word of God. We dream of families in this generation and the generations to come, worshipping Jesus Christ! in truth and in spirit, Mentorship and refreshment through entertainment guided by the principles in the word of God.

Mombasa soaking night

In this Night, we invite God to come and touch and love his people. This facilitates the presence of the Father,Son and Holy Spirit through annointed soaking music and waiting in His presence. There is an increase in His presence when we come together in one place to be with Him. For where two or three are gathered in my name, I am in the midst of them.(Matt. 18:19). This soaking night is for awakening who we are as the sons and dsughters of the loving father.It's a night of transformation , healing and discovering of the deep affirmation of our heavenly father.
